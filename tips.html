Top Five Productivity Essentials That Will Change The Game

Clear Goal

Sharpen your focus with clear, defined goals—think SMART: specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, and time-bound.


Plan Ahead

Plan for the day, You will end up doing things that are thrown at you externally. 'If you fail to plan, you are planning to fail!'- B. Franklin.


The ONE Thing

“What's the ONE Thing I can do, such that by doing it, everything else will be easier or unnecessary?"The One thing G.Keller & J. Papasan


No Disctractions

Cultivate a zone of focus by cutting out interruptions and creating a space where your attentionn can thrive.


Sleep

When you are tired you make bad chiooces, tasks take longer and health suffers. Aim for 7-8h regularly timmed sleep, no excuses.“Fatigue makes cowards of us all.“ V.Lombardi.
